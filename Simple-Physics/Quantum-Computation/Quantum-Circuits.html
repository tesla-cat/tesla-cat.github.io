What is the problem ?¶

  • Quantum Mechanics is nothing but Linear Algebra, so a Quantum Circuit is nothing but a Linear Operator acting on Qubits

  • 3 gates: H, T, CNOT, can approximate any Unitary Operator, we can also add S gate for fault tolerance
