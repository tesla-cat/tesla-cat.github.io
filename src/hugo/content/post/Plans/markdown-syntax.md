## Application materials

- transcripts
- Personal statement: maximum 400-500 words, concise
  - Your background: what's brought you to choose a PhD, NOT your life story
  - Why PhD: motivation 
  - Academic experience: comment on your CV, explain the significance of those qualifications for your PhD application
  - Extra-curricular experience: experience that has also demonstrated (or developed) relevant skills for your PhD, such as organisation, independent project management, self-motivation, etc.
  - Your broader goals and motivations: what you plan to do after your doctorate
- Letters of recommendation
- CV
