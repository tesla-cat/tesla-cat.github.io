## A Not Brief History of Rick

- `1997.11.08`: 
    I was born in China, Shaanxi province, Xianyang city, which was the capital city of the first united China.
    I didn't know what happened at this point.
- `1997-2004`:
    Awaiting archaeology.
- `2004-2010`:
    I studied at Xianyang Experimental Primary School.
    I stayed with my grandpa's family for most time of this period, during which I always have ShuiJianBao for breakfast.
    I remember that I always listened to songs and looked outside the window at night, watching the moon, missing my mother. 
    I can't remember where she was and what she was doing. I think I didn't get to see her for a very long time at then.
    Later my mother and father had a divorce. I moved to a place near the school and started living with my mother.
    I can't remember how she made money. She was always at home cooking meals for me.
    We lived in a poor condition, the first thing I recall every time about this period is the night when a 
    thief broke into our house from the kitchen. Luckily he didn't get through the kitchen door. 
    It could have changed my entire trajectory because there were no man to protect us.
    We moved to a safer area near by soon after, where I started doing science experiments, with whatever I could find,
    as I was suddenly extremely fascinated by the science class. 
    My mother bought me many books, including science book series by Yakov Perelman, they were my main source of my joy.
- `2010-2013`:
    I studied at Shaanxi University of Science & Technology Affiliated Middle School. 
- `2013-2015`:
    I studied at Xi'an Gaoxin No.1 High School, which was the best high school in Shaanxi province and ranked 8-th nationwide.
- `2015-2016`: 
    I was awarded a China-Singapore intergovernmental scholarship called Senior Middle 2 Scholarship. 
    This year I came to Singapore to attend a one year bridging course supervised by Prof. TAY Seng Chuan at National University of Singapore. 
    It was my first time going abroad.  
- `2016-2020`: 
    I finished the SM2 bridging course and entered NUS. I chose to study Physics without any hesitation.
- `2019`:
    I did exchange in Germany.
- `2020-2021`:
    I worked in Prof. Yvonne Gao's Quantum Computing group.
