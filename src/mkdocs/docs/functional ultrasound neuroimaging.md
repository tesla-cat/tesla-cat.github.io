
#  Functional ultrasound neuroimaging

## `Caltech`: Single-trial decoding of movement intentions using functional ultrasound neuroimaging

### hardwares

- `Verasonics` Vantage 128 Research Ultrasound System  
- `Vermon` DOMINO linear array probe

### introduction

- Introduced by (Mace, 2011)
- used to image neural activity during  
    - epileptic seizures (Mace, 2011)
    - olfactory 嗅觉 stimuli (Osmanski, 2014)
    - behavioral tasks in freely moving rodents with miniature, head-mountable transducers (Sieu, 2015; Urban, 2015)
    - ferrets (Bimbard, 2018)
    - pigeons (Rau, 2018)
    - non-human primates (Blaize, 2020; Dizeux, 2019)
    - humans (Demene, 2017; Imbault, 2017; Soloukey, 2020)
- 5 to 10 fold better in terms of spatiotemporal resolution and sensitivity compared to fMRI (Deffieux, 2018; Mace, 2011; Rabut, 2020)

### posterior parietal cortex (PPC)

- PPC is an association cortical region 
- situated between visual and motor cortical areas 
- involved in 
    - spatial attention (Colby and Goldberg, 1999)
    - multisensory integration (Andersen and Buneo, 2002)
    - sensorimotor transformations for movement planning (Andersen and Cui, 2009)
- subregions 
    - lateral intraparietal area (LIP): planning saccade movements (Andersen et al., 1987; Andersen and Buneo, 2002; Gnadt and Andersen, 1988; Snyder et al., 1997) 
    - parietal reach region (PRR): planning reach movements (Andersen and Buneo, 2002; Calton et al., 2002; Snyder et al., 2000, 1997). - Importantly, their activity can be simultaneously recorded within a single fUS imaging frame (12.8 x 13 x 0.4 mm)

### results

- miniaturized 15-MHz, linear array transducer 
- placed on the dura via a cranial window
- spatial resolution: 100 x 100 um in-plane, slice thicknesses of 400 um, covering a plane with a width of 12.8 mm and penetration depth of 16 mm
- We used a plane-wave imaging sequence at a pulse repetition frequency of 7,500 Hz and compounded frames collected from a 500-ms period each second to form power Doppler images with a 1 Hz refresh rate
